Message from CEO

Assalamu Alaikum, 
On the occasion of 16 th Founding Anniversary of United Hospital Limited I would like to Congratulate all our Physicians, Nurses, Allied health service workers and non-clinical staffs for being part of this wonderful journey with dedication and hard work that make United hospital the premium health care facility in the country. Congratulations to all of you!

The journey began in 2006 with the motto of providing the best possible health care support to the people of Bangladesh and to bring the world class health care within the reach of common people. After 16 years of operation and serving almost millions of patients, we have established ourselves as a trusted health care partner of the nation.
Our passionate staff have remained committed to the mission while been focused, motivated and responsive. We have faced many challenges, not the least of which have been the ever-evolving changes in the health care industry. Yet we continue to forge ahead to meet these changes and prepare for a new era in our history without compromising our mission.  United hospital is an example to what can be accomplished when there is a sincere desire to serve. A very WELL DONE to you.

It is an exciting time for us. In one hand, we are very close to go for JCI accreditation which will take our hospital to a whole different level of quality health care, on the hand I am truly delighted to let you know that we are growing bigger under the banner of United health care services from this year. with a vision to lead the development of healthcare solution for the people of Bangladesh and to provide affordable health care, apart from United Hospital, we now have state of art 250 bedded MA Rashid Hospital at Jamalpur and a comprehensive day care solution center named Medix with its 1st branch opened very recently at Dhanmondi. Under United health care services, we
have United College of Nursing and United Medical College to ensure that we have trained and skillful health care resources to support us in future.

United Hospital has been able to engrave itself in the hearts of our patients and all of that became possible for the hard work and dedication of our Doctors, Nurses and other staffs who have worked tirelessly day and night for all these years to save lives. I belief with the grace of Allah subhanahu wa ta’ala United health care services through all it’s concern will make a bigger impact in the health care industry of Bangladesh in coming
years where the demand of quality and affordable health care is very high and rising.

My sincere thanks and gratitude to all of you for being part of our journey whole heartedly. Together, we will achieve more success in coming days and years, Inshallah.

Stay safe, stay blessed.

Mohammad Faizur Rahman

CEO United Healthcare
