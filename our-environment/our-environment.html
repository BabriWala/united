Environment

Anthropocentrism – mother nature has been utilized, transformed, conquered, transcended and even dominated by mankind. We, the occupants of this planet, have simply accommodated ourselves to the environment, and endlessly demanded from nature what seemed to its abundant and inexhaustible resources to fulfill our desires. However, the emergence of warning signs from nature has given a wakeup call to the inhabitants. Since then, Sustainable Development has been the term that has been balancing environment and anthropocentrism so that society and economy can develop within carrying capacity.

United has adopted being a socially responsible corporate entity by supporting community development activities, hence is committed to socio-economic development of the country. We at United recognize that we have a responsibility to the environment beyond legal and regulatory requirement. We are committed to reducing environmental impact and continually improving our environmental performance as an integral part of our business strategy and operating methods.

United endeavors to:

  • Comply with all relevant regulatory requirements
  • Continually improve and monitor environmental performance
  • Continually improve and reduce environmental impacts
  • Incorporate environmental factors into business decisions
  • Increase employee awareness

Starting with designing efficient industries that require less fossil fuel consumption to implementing multi-stage processes, United has a long track record of providing pioneering environment friendly solutions for developing the nation’s infrastructure.
