Our contribution

United Trust patronizes around 27 schools and madrashas that educate more than 8000 students

UT operates 08 rural clinics / small hospitals where more than 1,75,000 outdoor patients are being treated annually and more than 6000 cataract operations are conducted per year, most of which are almost free of cost.

It runs interest free credit scheme to encourage self-employment; number of beneficiaries have exceeded 26,000 families.
